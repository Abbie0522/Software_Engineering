How to Use FitTrack

1️⃣

Schedule Your Workouts

Go to the Schedule page, click the "+" button on any day, select your exercise, set the duration, and save. Plan your entire week at a glance.

2️⃣

Track Daily Progress

Visit the Tracker page each day. Your scheduled workouts automatically appear. Click the checkmark to mark workouts as complete.

3️⃣

Monitor Your History

Check the History section in Tracker to see all your completed workouts. Watch your consistency grow over time.
